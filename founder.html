Amol Gajare

Founder & Director

Amol Gajare is the guiding force behind the company’s growth and long-term vision. As Director, he provides strategic leadership across all business verticals, ensuring that every solution delivered is efficient, sustainable, and aligned with industry best practices.

"Driven by a passion for sustainability, creating solutions that support cleaner environments and responsible industrial development."

With strong expertise in environmental engineering and turnkey project execution, he oversees the planning, development, and implementation of STP, ETP, OWC, and ZLD systems. His leadership is instrumental in maintaining high-quality standards, optimizing operations, and building trusted relationships with clients across sectors.

Amol is known for his proactive approach, attention to detail, and commitment to innovation. He ensures that the organization stays ahead with modern technologies, reliable service delivery, and transparent communication. Under his direction, the team continues to achieve consistent project performance and steady organizational expansion.
