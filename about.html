Our Story

Welcome to Enversys Greentek Solutions

Based in Pune and serving clients across India, we are a leading manufacturing and consultancy firm boasting extensive expertise in industrial pollution control and statutory compliance. We pride ourselves on being a highly competent organization and your one-stop solution for all industrial pollution abatement needs.

Our experts bring diverse experience in designing treatment plants tailored to different industries. Our competent staff—including Process Design Engineers, Project Commissioning Engineers, Chemists, and Operators—ensures seamless project execution.

Over the years, we have completed numerous projects encompassing the design, installation, and commissioning of cutting-edge wastewater treatment plants (UFRP, WTP, STP, ETP & ZLD) and air pollution control equipment. We also specialize in Environmental Consultancy Services, providing expertise in EC, EIA, EMP, LEED, IGBC & GRIHA certifications, as well as Carbon footprint studies and Green Audits.
