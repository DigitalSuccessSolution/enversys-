Our Key Services

Comprehensive solutions for reporting, compliance, and sustainable development.

ESG & Sustainability Reporting

Reporting as per International Standards (GRI, SASB, TCFD, CDP) & preparation of BRSR (Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting).

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)

Assessment of products and services (Cradle to Gate/Grave) and Environmental Product Declaration (EPD) Certification.

Green Building Certification

Consultancy and compliance for LEED, IGBC, Eco Housing, and GRIHA certifications.

GHG Accounting & Reporting

Comprehensive Greenhouse Gas (GHG) inventory, accounting, and reporting services.

CBAM Advisory

Guidance on Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) for international trade compliance.

Net Zero & SBTi

Development of Net Zero Strategies, Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) Target Setting & Advisory.

Policies & Frameworks

Development of customized ESG/Sustainability Policies & operational Frameworks.

Building Simulation

Advanced Building Performance Simulation to optimize energy efficiency and design.

Energy & Water Audit

Detailed Energy Audits, Water Audits, and comprehensive Sustainability Reporting.
