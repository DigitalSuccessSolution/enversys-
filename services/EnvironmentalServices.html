Our Expertise

Comprehensive Environmental Solutions

We provide end-to-end environmental consultancy services to ensure your projects meet all regulatory requirements while promoting sustainable development. From initial clearances to post-project monitoring, we are your trusted partners.

Project Clearances

Facilitate and Obtaining Environmental Clearances for All Applicable Projects.

EIA Studies

Comprehensive Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) Studies for new and expansion projects.

CRZ Clearances

Specialized Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) Clearances for coastal projects.

Forest Clearances

Guidance and processing for obtaining necessary Forest Clearances.

Post-EC Compliances

Managing Post-Environmental Clearance Compliances to ensure ongoing adherence.

MoEF&CC Reports

Certified Compliance Reports from the Regional Office of MoEF&CC.

EMP Preparation

Preparation of detailed Environmental Management Plans (EMP) for sustainable operations.

CEMP Preparation

Construction Environmental Management Plan (CEMP) for the construction phase of projects.

Due Diligence

Environmental and Social Due Diligence (Phase I & Phase II) for mergers and acquisitions.

Green Audits

Environmental Site Auditing and Green Audits to assess environmental performance.

Remediation

Environmental Remediation Assessment for contaminated sites.

Statement Reports

Preparation of Environmental Statement & Compliance Reports.

Pollution Certification

"No Increase in Pollution Load" Certification services.

Solid Waste

Preparation of comprehensive Solid Waste Management Plans.

Disaster Management

Preparation of Disaster Management Plans for industrial safety.
