Water Solutions

Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

The STP treats domestic wastewater using biological and mechanical processes. It produces clean, reusable water, helping reduce overall water consumption.

Industrial Solutions

Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

The ETP handles industrial effluents and removes pollutants through advanced treatment stages. It ensures all discharged water meets environmental norms.

Advanced Technology

Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD)

Our ZLD system ensures that no liquid waste leaves the facility. It recycles maximum water and converts remaining waste into safe solid by-products.

Efficiency

Multi Effect Evaporator (MEE)

The MEE system concentrates wastewater using multiple evaporation stages. It improves efficiency and reduces overall waste volume for further processing.

Green Energy

Organic Waste Converter (OWC)

The OWC converts biodegradable waste into nutrient-rich compost. This supports green landscaping and promotes sustainable waste management.

About Enversys

Leading Environmental Consultancy & Manufacturing Firm

Welcome to Enversys Greentek Solutions, a leading manufacturing & consultancy firm based in Pune, serving clients across India. We are your one-stop solution for all industrial pollution abatement needs.

With extensive expertise in statutory compliance and pollution control, we specialize in designing tailored treatment plants and handling the manufacturing, operation, and maintenance of WTP, STP, ETP, ZLD, and Biogas plants. Our mission is to provide innovative, economic, and compliant solutions while ensuring pollution prevention.
