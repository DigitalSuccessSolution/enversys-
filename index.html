About Enversys

Leading Environmental Consultancy & Manufacturing Firm

Welcome to Enversys Greentek Solutions, a leading manufacturing & consultancy firm based in Pune, serving clients across India. We are your one-stop solution for all industrial pollution abatement needs.

With extensive expertise in statutory compliance and pollution control, we specialize in designing tailored treatment plants and handling the manufacturing, operation, and maintenance of WTP, STP, ETP, ZLD, and Biogas plants. Our mission is to provide innovative, economic, and compliant solutions while ensuring pollution prevention.
